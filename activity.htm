Thursday Night Bingo

Every Thursday evening we are hosting BINGO to benefit the Volunteer Center at Westchester Hall, located at 2801 F Street.

Warm Ups start at 5 p.m., with Early Birds at 6 p.m. and Regular Games at 6:30 p.m.

There will be special giveaways/door prizes every night, with the Early Bird and Regular Games jackpots at $250

Support local volunteer efforts and have a fun time doing it playing Bingo.

Call 395-9787 for more info.

Volunteers floor workers and Bingo callers are needed each week.
